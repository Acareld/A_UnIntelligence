# Artificial UnIntelligence

**A downloadable prototype game by Team Glados**  
Developed for **DES502 – Game Design and Development**

## What is Artificial UnIntelligence?

- Set in a futuristic, cyberpunk-inspired world
- Robots are here to help, so humans no longer have to work
- Infrastructure is now built and maintained by robots
- You play as an expendable safety inspection robot
- Your goal is to survey the area and declare it safe for human use
- Interact with different objects and environments to discover what is, and is not, a hazard
- You may die, but that is just part of the job

## How to Play

Declare the environment safe for human use by discovering as many potential hazards as possible.

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| W / A / S / D | Movement |
| E | Interact |

Keybinds can be changed in-game.
